How does it work?

Create your own profile, send and receive language partner requests from users around the globe, video chat and record your conversations, re-listen to your conversations to improve your pronounciation and understanding.

But what if I don't yet speak a word of the language I want to learn?

No worries! You can send messages to your partner in your native language and they'll see a translation of your message as well as the original. You even can annotate your recordings with timed comments.
